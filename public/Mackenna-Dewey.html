Common Ground

Coffee That Cares

Common Ground is a series of documentaries about on local coffee roasters that focus on responsible sourcing and high quality coffee. Carabello and La Terza make sure that their impact is a positive one, literally from the ground up. Common Ground introduces these roasters to the Cincinnati community.
