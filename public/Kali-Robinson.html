The Lazy Ass Beginner's Guide to Going Vegetarian

Eating a vegetarian diet is great for you and the environment. In order to sustain a balanced vegetarian diet you have to learn how to cook for yourself. Young adults understand the benefits of cooking at home, but feel lost in the kitchen. This book makes cooking at home easier with uniquely designed, easy to follow recipes.
