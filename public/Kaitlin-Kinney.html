Pesky Nomad

A Cultural Awareness Guide for the Uninformed American

When traveling across Europe, college age Americans overlook research into the cultural nuances of countries they plan to visit. By educating these travelers on the top five basic cultural differences between each country they intend to visit, a large amount of frustration can be saved for all parties involved.
