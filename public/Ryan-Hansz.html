Stressit App

Empowering college students to get better at stress.

College is full of new experiences that students have to deal with on their own. Based on techniques from cognitive behavioral therapy and behavioral design, this application acknowledges the pressure filled reality of college and helps students take small steps to feel in control of their mind.
