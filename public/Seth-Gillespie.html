PreMeds

A tablet application addressing the dangers of opioid pain medication

Most pharmacies fail to provide clear information to patients regarding the health risks of opioid pain medications. Many patients remain ignorant of how to take their pain medication safely, which can lead to major health issues. PreMeds aims to educate patients about their opioid pain medication to empower them to safely treat their pain.
